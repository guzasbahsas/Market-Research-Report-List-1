<p><p>The Air Bag Opening Valve market comprises two main types: Continuous Airbag Opening Valves and Metering Bag Opening Valves. Continuous Airbag Opening Valves facilitate a steady flow of gas into the airbag, ensuring immediate and consistent inflation during deployment. In contrast, Metering Bag Opening Valves control the inflation process by regulating the gas flow, allowing for precise inflation tailored to specific crash conditions. Both types enhance safety by ensuring airbags deploy effectively in automotive applications, responding to varying impact scenarios.</p></p>
