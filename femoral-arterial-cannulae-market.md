<p><p>The femoral arterial cannulae market is categorized into unipolar and bipolar types, each serving specific clinical purposes. Unipolar femoral arterial cannulae are designed for single-direction blood flow, commonly used in procedures requiring temporary perfusion support. In contrast, bipolar femoral arterial cannulae enable bidirectional flow, facilitating more complex interventions such as cardiac surgeries. Both types are crucial for ensuring effective circulation and oxygenation during medical procedures, driving advancements in design and materials for enhanced patient outcomes.</p></p>
