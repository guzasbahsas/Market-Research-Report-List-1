<p><p>The Smart Windows market includes two primary types: Active Glasses and Passive Glasses. Active Glasses utilize electronic technologies, such as liquid crystals or electrochromic materials, to adjust transparency and tint dynamically, responding to environmental changes for energy efficiency and privacy. Conversely, Passive Glasses primarily rely on static coatings or materials that block UV and infrared light but do not change based on external conditions. Both play a crucial role in enhancing building automation, comfort, and energy savings.</p></p>
