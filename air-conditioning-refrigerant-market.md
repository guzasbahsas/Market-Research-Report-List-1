<p><p>The air conditioning refrigerant market includes several key types: R-134A, a hydrofluorocarbon (HFC) commonly used in automotive AC; R-32, a more environmentally friendly HFC with lower global warming potential; R-407, a blend of HFCs designed as a replacement for R-22; and R-410A, a popular choice for residential units due to its efficiency yet relatively high GWP.
