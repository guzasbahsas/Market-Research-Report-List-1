<p><p>The Multi-Guide Sleep Monitor market consists of three primary types: EGG (Electroencephalogram), Dynamic, and Video monitoring. EGG utilizes electrodes to measure brain activity during sleep, providing insights into various sleep stages. Dynamic monitoring involves wearable devices that track physiological parameters such as heart rate and movement, offering a holistic view of sleep quality. Video monitoring employs cameras to observe sleep behaviors visually, allowing for the analysis of sleep patterns and disturbances, enhancing overall understanding of sleep health.</p></p>
