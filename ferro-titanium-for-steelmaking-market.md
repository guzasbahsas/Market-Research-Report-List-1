FeTi40 contains approximately 40% titanium and is often used for low alloy steel applications, offering cost efficiency. In contrast, FeTi70 has around 70% titanium, providing superior strength and is preferred in high-performance steel applications, particularly in aerospace and automotive industries, where enhanced mechanical properties are critical.</p></p>
