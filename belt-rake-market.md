<p><p>Belt rake markets can be categorized into center delivery and side delivery systems. In a center delivery market, the conveyor belt transports materials to a central rake, which then distributes the product evenly across a designated area. Conversely, in a side delivery market, the conveyor belt moves materials to the side, allowing for lateral distribution. Each system offers specific advantages in terms of space utilization and operational efficiency, catering to varying industrial needs.</p></p>
