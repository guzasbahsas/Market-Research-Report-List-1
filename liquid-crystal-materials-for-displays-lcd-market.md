<p><p>Liquid crystal materials are essential for various types of LCD displays, each serving specific applications. TFT (Thin Film Transistor) materials offer high-resolution and fast response times, ideal for modern screens. TN (Twisted Nematic) materials provide simple, cost-effective displays with moderate image quality and viewing angles. STN (Super Twisted Nematic) materials enhance contrast and viewing angles, suitable for mobile devices. HTN (High Twisted Nematic) materials improve color reproduction and response, catering to high-performance applications. Each type serves diverse market needs.</p></p>
