<p><ul><li>Hard Clay</li><li>Soft Clay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refractory clay market can be categorized into two primary types: hard clay and soft clay. Hard clay, characterized by its high alumina content and excellent strength, is used in high-temperature applications such as kiln linings and furnace components. Soft clay, with a lower alumina content, is more plastic and easier to mold, making it suitable for applications like ceramics and insulators. Both types serve vital roles in industries requiring materials that can withstand extreme heat and mechanical stresses.</p></p>
