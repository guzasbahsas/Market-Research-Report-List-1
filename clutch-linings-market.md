<p><p>The clutch linings market is segmented into various types, including calendering glue and impregnation drying tower processes. Calendering glue involves applying adhesive to layered materials, enhancing bonding strength and ensuring durability in clutch applications. In contrast, impregnation drying tower processes involve saturating the lining materials with resin compounds and then drying them in specialized towers to create a robust, heat-resistant finish. Both methods contribute to the overall performance and longevity of clutch linings in automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
