<p><p>Heart defect closure devices are specialized medical instruments designed to correct various congenital or acquired heart defects. Types include ASD (Atrial Septal Defect) closure devices for sealing holes between heart chambers, VSD (Ventricular Septal Defect) closure devices for similar purposes in the ventricles, PDA (Patent Ductus Arteriosus) closure devices to close abnormal connections between major vessels, PFO (Patent Foramen Ovale) closure devices for sealing openings between atria, and LAA (Left Atrial Appendage) closure devices to prevent blood clots in the heart. Each plays a critical role in improving cardiac function and reducing complications.</p></p>
