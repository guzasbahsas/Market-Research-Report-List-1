<p><p>The floor polishing tools market is divided into two main types: dry polishing and wet polishing. Dry polishing employs specialized pads and machines to buff surfaces without water, resulting in minimal mess and quicker drying times. It's often preferred for convenience and speed. Wet polishing, on the other hand, utilizes water to lubricate the surface, reducing dust and enhancing the finish. This method is ideal for achieving a high-gloss shine on hard surfaces, making it popular for residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
