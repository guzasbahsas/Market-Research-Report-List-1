<p><strong>The Truck Tyre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck tire market is primarily divided into two types: radial and bias tires. Radial tires feature cords arranged at a 90-degree angle to the tread, providing improved fuel efficiency, better tread wear, and enhanced stability. They are increasingly popular for long-haul applications. Bias tires, on the other hand, have cords that crisscross in diagonal layers, offering stronger sidewalls and a more rugged design, which can be beneficial for off-road conditions. Each type serves distinct performance needs in the trucking industry.</p></p>
