<p><strong>The Hip Reconstruction Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Cemented Hip Replacement</li><li>Primary Cementless Hip Replacement</li><li>Partial Hip Replacement</li><li>Revision Hip Replacement</li><li>Hip Resurfacing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hip reconstruction market includes several types of procedures. Primary cemented hip replacement involves fixing the implant with bone cement for stability. Primary cementless hip replacement relies on bone growth into the implant for fixation. Partial hip replacement replaces only the femoral head, typically in older patients. Revision hip replacement addresses complications from previous surgeries. Hip resurfacing conserves bone by covering the femoral head with a metal cap, ideal for younger patients requiring hip joint repair.</p></p>
