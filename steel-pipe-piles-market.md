<p><p>The steel pipe piles market encompasses various types of construction technologies, primarily including Spiral Weld Pipe, Electric Resistance Weld (ERW), and Double Submerged Arc Weld (DSAW). Spiral Weld Pipe is characterized by a helical seam, making it suitable for large-diameter applications. Electric Resistance Weld uses high-frequency currents to join edges, offering efficiency for thinner pipes. DSAW involves welding two seams simultaneously underwater, ensuring robust structural integrity. Each type caters to specific engineering requirements and project specifications, influencing construction practices.</p></p>
