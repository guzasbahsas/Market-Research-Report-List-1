<p><p>The Human Immunodeficiency Virus (HIV) rapid test kits market includes two primary types: Rapid Immunoassay Tests and Rapid Molecular Diagnostic Tests. </p><p>Rapid Immunoassay Tests utilize antibodies to detect HIV antibodies or antigens in a patient’s sample, providing results within minutes. In contrast, Rapid Molecular Diagnostic Tests detect the genetic material of HIV, offering higher sensitivity and specificity. Both types are crucial for early diagnosis and treatment, facilitating timely clinical decisions and improving patient care in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
