<p><strong>The Embolic Protection Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distal Filter Devices</li><li>Distal Occlusion Devices</li><li>Proximal Occlusion Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Embolic protection devices (EPDs) are critical in interventional cardiology and peripheral vascular procedures, designed to prevent emboli from entering the bloodstream during interventions. The market comprises three main types: Distal Filter Devices, which capture debris before it can reach the distal vasculature; Distal Occlusion Devices, which block blood flow at the site to prevent emboli from traveling; and Proximal Occlusion Devices, which occlude the vessel upstream to protect the distal area during procedures, ensuring safer interventions and improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
