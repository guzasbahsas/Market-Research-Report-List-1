<p><strong>The Semi-Automatic Vacuum Packaging Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>V-FFS</li><li>H-FFS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-automatic vacuum packaging machine market is primarily divided into two types: Vertical Form-Fill-Seal (V-FFS) and Horizontal Form-Fill-Seal (H-FFS). V-FFS machines operate vertically, creating bags from a roll of film, suitable for products like powders and granules. In contrast, H-FFS machines operate horizontally, filling pre-formed pouches, ideal for larger or bulk items. Both types enhance product shelf life by removing air, improving packing efficiency, and are widely used in food, pharmaceuticals, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
