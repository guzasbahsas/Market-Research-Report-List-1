<p><ul><li>Self-erecting Tower Cranes</li><li>Flat Top Tower Cranes</li><li>Hammerhead Tower Cranes</li><li>Luffing Jib Tower Cranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small tower crane market comprises several types, each suited for specific construction needs. Self-erecting tower cranes are compact and easily assembled on-site, ideal for smaller projects. Flat top tower cranes offer a modular design with no tower obstruction, making them versatile for urban settings. Hammerhead tower cranes feature a horizontal jib and are effective for heavy lifting. Luffing jib tower cranes can operate in confined spaces with a flexible design that allows the jib to lift loads at various angles, optimizing space and efficiency.</p></p>
