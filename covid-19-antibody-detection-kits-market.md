<p><p>The COVID-19 antibody detection kits market includes various testing methodologies: </p><p>1. **Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)** - These are point-of-care tests that provide quick results, typically within 15-30 minutes, detecting antibodies with minimal equipment. </p><p>2. **Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)** - A lab-based test that offers higher sensitivity and specificity, used for comprehensive antibody quantification. </p><p>3. **Neutralization Assay** - This evaluates the effectiveness of antibodies in blocking virus infection, providing insights into immune response and potential vaccine efficacy. Each method caters to different diagnostic needs and settings.</p></p>
