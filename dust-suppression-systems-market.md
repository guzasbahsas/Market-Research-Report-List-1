<p><p>The dust suppression systems market consists of two primary types: dry and wet dust suppression systems. Dry dust suppression systems utilize barriers, air filtration, and specialized particles to capture and control airborne dust without adding moisture. In contrast, wet dust suppression systems spray water or chemical solutions to dampen dust particles, preventing them from becoming airborne. Both systems are essential in various industries, such as mining, construction, and demolition, to enhance air quality and ensure worker safety while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
