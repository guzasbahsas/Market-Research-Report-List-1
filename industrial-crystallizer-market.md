<p><strong>The Industrial Crystallizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Evaporative Crystallizers</li><li>Cooling Crystallizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial crystallizer market is primarily divided into two main types: evaporative crystallizers and cooling crystallizers. Evaporative crystallizers operate by concentrating a solution through heat application, leading to the precipitation of solids as the solvent evaporates. In contrast, cooling crystallizers function by lowering the temperature of a solution, promoting crystallization as solubility decreases. Both types are crucial in various industries for the production of high-purity materials and efficient separation of solid substances from liquid solutions.</p></p>
