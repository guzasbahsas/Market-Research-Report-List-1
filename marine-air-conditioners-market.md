<p><strong>The Marine Air Conditioners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unitary Type</li><li>Split Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine air conditioners market includes two primary types: unitary and split systems. Unitary types consist of a single compact unit that combines both cooling and heating functions, making installation simpler and more space-efficient. In contrast, split types feature two separate components: an indoor unit for air distribution and an outdoor unit for heat exchange. This configuration allows for more flexibility in installation and can offer quieter operation, making them preferable for larger vessels requiring efficient climate control solutions.</p></p>
