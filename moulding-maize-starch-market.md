<p><p>The moulding maize starch market can be segmented into three main types: Rapidly Digestible Starch (RDS), Slowly Digestible Starch (SDS), and Resistant Starch (RS). RDS is quickly broken down into glucose, providing fast energy. SDS digests more slowly, offering sustained energy release, which is beneficial for blood sugar management. RS resists digestion in the small intestine, acting as dietary fiber, promoting gut health. Each type caters to different consumer needs, influencing their applications across food, nutrition, and health sectors.</p></p>
