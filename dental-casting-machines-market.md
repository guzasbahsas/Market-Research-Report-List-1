<p><ul><li>Induction Casting Machine</li><li>Centrifugal Casting Machine</li><li>Vacuum Casting Machine</li><li>Electric Arc Casting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental casting machines market comprises several types that cater to specific casting needs. Induction casting machines use electromagnetic induction to melt metal alloys, ensuring precise temperature control. Centrifugal casting machines utilize centrifugal force to distribute molten metal evenly, enhancing density and reducing porosity. Vacuum casting machines operate under reduced pressure to minimize gas inclusion during casting, leading to superior surface finishes. Electric arc casting machines employ an electric arc to melt materials quickly, providing high-quality castings with great accuracy.</p></p>
