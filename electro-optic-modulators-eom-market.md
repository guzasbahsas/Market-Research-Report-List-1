The market comprises various types: polarization modulators adjust the light's polarization state; amplitude modulators vary the light's intensity; and phase modulators manipulate the phase to encode information. Other EOM types include frequency modulators and dual-function devices. These modulators are essential in telecommunications, sensors, and laser technologies, enabling advancements in data transmission, imaging, and signal processing across multiple applications and industries.</p></p>
