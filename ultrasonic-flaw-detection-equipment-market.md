<p><ul><li>Conventional Ultrasonic Flaw Detection Equipment</li><li>Phased Array Ultrasonic Flaw Detection Equipment</li><li>TOFD Ultrasonic Flaw Detection Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic flaw detection equipment is crucial for identifying internal defects in materials. The market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Conventional Ultrasonic Flaw Detection Equipment** relies on single-element probes to transmit and receive ultrasound waves, offering basic flaw detection capabilities. </p><p>2. **Phased Array Ultrasonic Flaw Detection Equipment** utilizes multiple elements to steer ultrasound beams, enhancing image quality and defect characterization. </p><p>3. **Time-of-Flight Diffraction (TOFD) Ultrasonic Flaw Detection Equipment** measures the time taken for sound waves to bounce back, providing precise depth measurements of flaws in materials.</p></p>
