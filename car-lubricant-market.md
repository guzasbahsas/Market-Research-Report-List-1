<p><p>The car lubricant market encompasses various types essential for vehicle performance, including engine oil, gear oil, and transmission fluid. Engine oil reduces friction and wear in the engine, ensuring smooth operation and longevity. Gear oil is formulated to protect gears and bearings in the transmission, enhancing efficiency and preventing metal-to-metal contact. Transmission fluid serves as a lubricant and coolant in automatic transmissions, enabling seamless gear shifts and prolonging system life. Each type plays a critical role in maintaining vehicle functionality.</p></p>
