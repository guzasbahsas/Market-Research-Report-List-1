<p><p>The flangeless V port ball valves market encompasses two primary types: soft seal and hard seal valves. Soft seal valves feature elastomeric seals that provide a tight closure, ideal for applications requiring bubble-tight sealing and versatility at lower pressures. In contrast, hard seal valves utilize metal-to-metal contact, offering durability and resistance to high temperatures and pressures, making them suitable for demanding industrial environments. Both types cater to various sectors, including oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing, ensuring efficient flow control.</p></p>
