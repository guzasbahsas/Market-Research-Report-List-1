<p><p>Smart home speakers are categorized primarily into Wi-Fi enabled and Bluetooth enabled types. Wi-Fi enabled speakers connect to home networks, offering access to streaming services, voice assistants, and smart home controls, enabling multi-room audio capabilities. They require a stable internet connection for optimal performance. In contrast, Bluetooth enabled speakers provide a simpler connection method, allowing users to stream audio directly from smartphones or tablets without the need for a Wi-Fi network. This makes them portable and versatile for on-the-go use.</p></p>
