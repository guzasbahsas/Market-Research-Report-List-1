<p><strong>The Modular Gripper Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Gripper Systems</li><li>Angular Gripper Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Modular gripper systems are essential for automation in various industries, with two primary types. Parallel gripper systems feature jaws that move parallel to each other, providing a stable grip suitable for handling flat or regular-shaped objects. Angular gripper systems, on the other hand, utilize jaws that pivot outward, allowing for a wider range of gripping angles, making them ideal for irregularly shaped or delicate items. Both types enhance operational efficiency and adaptability in robotic applications.</p></p>
