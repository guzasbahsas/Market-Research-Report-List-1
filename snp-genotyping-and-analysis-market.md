<p><p>SNP genotyping and analysis encompass various techniques to identify single nucleotide polymorphisms (SNPs) in genetic material. Dynamic Allele-Specific Hybridization utilizes allele-specific probes and real-time PCR for precise SNP detection. Molecular Beacons are fluorescent probes that emit signals upon hybridization with target SNPs, enabling real-time analysis. SNP microarrays leverage high-throughput technology to profile multiple SNPs simultaneously on a chip, providing comprehensive genotyping. Together, these methods enhance genomic research, personalized medicine, and disease association studies.</p></p>
