<p><p>Thermoplastic ball valves are categorized into several types based on their connection methods. Union ball valves feature a joinable design for easy assembly and disassembly, making maintenance straightforward. Flanged ball valves utilize flat surfaces and bolt holes for secure connections, ideal for high-pressure applications. Threaded ball valves have tapered threads that provide leak-proof joins without additional sealing, suitable for low-pressure systems. Other types may include welded or clamp-style valves, each offering unique benefits for specific industrial applications.</p></p>
