<p><p>The automotive sunroof motor market primarily features two types of motors: DC brushed motors and DC brushless motors. DC brushed motors are traditional, known for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, but they require maintenance due to wear on brushes. In contrast, DC brushless motors offer higher efficiency, longer lifespan, and reduced noise, making them increasingly popular for modern vehicles. As automotive technology evolves, the preference for brushless motors is rising, driven by demands for performance and energy efficiency in sunroof systems.</p></p>
