<p><p>The Desktop Drying Oven market is primarily categorized into two types: Heat Drying and Air Drying. Heat Drying utilizes controlled temperature to effectively remove moisture from materials, ensuring rapid and uniform drying. It is ideal for applications requiring consistent heating. Air Drying, on the other hand, relies on airflow to evaporate moisture, making it suitable for delicate or heat-sensitive materials. Both methods offer distinct advantages in applications such as laboratories, clinics, and small-scale industrial processes.</p></p>
